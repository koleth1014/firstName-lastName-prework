About Me

I am one who likes to move. I move and groove like water falling from a waterfall. Fluid movements are key, and thus, that is what I do. I am an enigma. I am myself and myself is I. I am one with myself. I become no other, but myself. I am me and no one else. Move and groove, wiggle through the vibrations. That is me. Many do not understand the ways of movement. One must become united in spirit, body, and mind. If the body is ready, the spirt is, and so the mind, then movement will happen. Music is the aid, the booster, that shines light into movement. If one can feel the music, they will surly feel the movements. And with those movements, will come the groove. With the groove, the wiggles will come as well. This, is the art of dance. One I choose to do. One I enjoy to do.
